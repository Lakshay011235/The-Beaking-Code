Insights:-
1. Green 4 U:-
In this platform our main focus will be on services which are providing the climate tech solutions with Users as companies.
Under this we have three sections(for now):-
(a). Green Book (a carbon foot print report generator) (b). Green Link (Linking the companies with foreign top market players)
(for getting services like Power supply, waste water management, air filters,...)
(c). Green Save (Understands you business process and suggest the product by which you reduce carbon emission)

2. Eco Store:-
In this platform we are going to create a dedicated store only for eco-friendly products so that user may not get confused about the product's contribution in saving the environment. Under this we have two sections(for now):-
(a). New Store (Managed by Drop shipping at initial stage)
(b). Reused Store (Has reused and recycled items via user-user selling and buying).

3. Eco Info:-
Through this platform we can achieve our main goal of creating a eco-friendly and sustainable future by creating awareness among people via Blogs and videos. For those who want to take a step ahead and willing to give ideas for this will reach our community seciton.

4. Campaigns:-
Outreaching of our Saas(Software as a solution) in large scale is mainly done by our Campaigns. Our Campaigns are like plant a tree on earthday, Electricity off(i.e. save as much Electricity as you can),...
